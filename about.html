Welcome to Pupils' Perspective

We work together on various social issues to make a positive impact in our community. We are a youth organization committed to social change and community development. Our mission is to empower young people to take initiative in solving social issues. Justice is our first priority.

Pupils' Perspective was established in March 2025. Our first project was the DU 2nd Time Re-open Movement. We're still working on this issue and hope to succeed very soon, In Sha Allah!
